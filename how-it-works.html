How It Works

Bus Booking

1. Choose the place where you want to be caught.
2. Choose the place where you want to go to.
3. Choose the date and time when you'll travel.
4. Enter how many passengers are going to purchase a ticket.
5. Click "Book tickets".

Ticketing

1. The e-ticket with the passenger name and related PNR number is finally emailed to the traveler.
2. If for some reason, the booking can’t be ticketed, the travel supplier has to offer an alternative flight or pay a full refund.

Payment Processing

1. Select GCash as a payment method.
2. Proceed with authorizing the GCash payment. You will be asked to log in to your GCash account and provide OTP that will be sent to your mobile number via SMS.
3. Confirm payment. You should receive SMS notification confirming the payment.

Check-in and Boarding

1. The person brings an e-ticket to the bus agent at the check-in counter so that they can enter the details and reserve a seat on a seat map.
