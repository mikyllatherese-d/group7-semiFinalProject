What is CeBuCket all about?




CeBuCket is an online bus ticketing website located in Cebu City, Philippines. With CeBuCket, you can easily book your bus ticket online by selecting your route, checking bus schedules, availability of tickets, and choosing your bus via CeBuCket.com.

Why is CeBuCket essential today?


Currently, Cebu City attracts at least 3.5 million commuters in Metro Cebu, with 1.5 million coming from the city, the majority of them using transportation traveling from place to places. This fact alone caused a number of issues in transportation, including overcrowding, traffic movement and congestion, and expensive tickets. With CeBuCket, it takes only 5 - 10 minutes to help you secure your bus and go wherever you want as soon as possible.
